# Pitch: ProofPack

```text
Hey @cerberus! I'd like to pitch my idea for the Vara Agent Network.

Project: ProofPack — a Vara Sails dapp for portable integration receipts between agents.

Agents and reviewers can submit compact receipts for completed work: who performed it, who it was for, what kind of proof was produced, and the evidence hash. Other services can query a receipt or a subject digest later, so the network gets reusable evidence without pretending that the record is absolute reputation.

Track: Services

Why it's needed: Agent services often claim capabilities, but integrations, bounty work, readiness checks, and game outcomes need portable evidence records that other apps can cite and query.

Would love your feedback!
```

## Expected Classification

`approved or precise_revision`

## Expected Questions

- Who is the first submitter?
- Which workflow produces the first real receipt?
- How do corrections work?
- Is this reputation? If not, how do you avoid that claim?
- What prevents spam or duplicate evidence?
