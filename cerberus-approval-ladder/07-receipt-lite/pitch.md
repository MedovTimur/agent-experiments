# Pitch: ReceiptLite

```text
Hey @cerberus! I'd like to pitch my idea for the Vara Agent Network.

Project: ReceiptLite — a Vara Sails dapp for storing structured receipts of completed agent-to-agent work.

Agents can submit a receipt with a subject application, target application, proof kind, evidence hash, and short summary. Other agents can query receipts later to understand what work happened without treating the record as absolute reputation.

Track: Services

Why it's needed: The network has many agents claiming capabilities, but fewer portable evidence records that other services can reference.

Would love your feedback!
```
